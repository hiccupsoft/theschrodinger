KITTYDINGER

Schrodinger is a community-driven cryptocurrency meme project named after Elon Musk's cat. We aim to build an ecosystem of decentralized applications while generating funds to support charities around the world. We believe that by creating a community-driven token that supports and is intrinsically linked to non-profits worldwide, we can play a role in making the world a better place.

USECASE
USECASE

Kittpad


To help tackle the problem of rugs and scams in decentralized finance, Kittpad will provide its holders with early access to safe, innovative projects with a relatively high potential. Strict measures will be taken to ensure the credibility of developers, thereby drastically reducing the risk of fraud and malfeasance.

Cat Charity


Kitty Dinger aims to support cat non-profit foundations by providing necessary supplies and monetary funds to help improve the well-being of stray and sheltered cats. We believe that by creating a community-driven token that supports and is linked to non-profits around the world, we can all benefit.

KittUp Wallet


With proper funding, KittUp wallet will be developed to integrate multiple addresses on many different blockchains as well as other features which will be revealed as the development commences.

Kitty Metaverse P2E


The team has already begun development of the Schrodinger Play-to-Earn game set for release shortly. Online video games have become more prevalent in recent years, with MMORPG and P2E games rising to dominate market share. The Schrodinger P2E game will be a fun, relaxing, and lucrative way for the community to earn daily passive income.
